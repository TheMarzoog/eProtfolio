Saudi Culture Research

In the Oral Communication course during my sophomore year, we were required to research one aspect of the Saudi Culture of our choosing. For this research, the instructor put the class in groups of three. Each group has to write a research paper and make a presentation. Our group chooses to research Saudi Weddings.

Reflection

To meet the requirements each team member picked a specific point of the research. One did the weddings in the past, another one did marriage at a young age, and I did Consanguinity marriage. To write the paper we google docs and each member wrote his part. In the end, we met and wrote the introduction and the conclusion. For the presentation, we used Prezi to make the slides and similar to the writing part each member worked on his part. From this assignment, I learned how to use technology to enhance my teamwork skills.
