Informative Presentation

In this assignment a was asked to make an informative presentation about any topic of my choice. Since it was informative, I chose I topic that I am familiar with. Photography equipment more specifically cameras and lenses. Each student was given five minutes to print.

Reflection

The Issue that I faced in this assignment is time. I found five minutes too short to inform the audience. Since I had 9 years of experience in photography, I thought that it was essential to talk about cameras and lenses in detail. Fortunately, I had an amazing instructor who helps me to narrow down the topic. So, in the end, I decided to talk just about the basic technical aspic of them. Thus, I just explain the difference between each type of camera and what are they use for. and I did the same thing for lenses. I learned from doing this presentation how to simplify advance ideas and teach them to an audience. Also, how to get their attention by change my voice note and using body language. Lastly, this presentation made me more confident in a public speech.
