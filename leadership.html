University App project

In software engineering course the final project was building an android university application. In this project, I was given the role of team leader of a team of 6. To complete this project, we need to complete two tasks. Having a working application and writing documentation for it.

Reflection

This assignment taught me how to divide the tasks for the team. I did it by dividing the team into two teams. Development team, which was led by me. This team was in charge of developing the software and testing it. Documentation team, which was led by a fellow student. Each team consists of 3 members. Every day I talk to the other leader to see the progress and report our advancement in the project. This assignment taught me how to use GitHub and agile practices to achieve my final goal.

Purpose, Culture, Opportunity & Brand

Like the bereaves assignment this one was a group assignment. Also, the instructor talked briefly about the case.

Reflection

This assignment taught me to step up and lead the team. In the beginning, the work was not organized and each member was working on his own. So, I decided to manage the work by doing two things. I created a Trello board with the tasks written on it. Then I told each member to pick a task. The second thing is a shared google document to write our notes. I learn from this assignment, how to manage and interact with a team remotely and effectively.

Saudi Culture Research

In the Oral Communication course during my sophomore year, we were required to research one aspect of the Saudi Culture of our choosing. For this research, the instructor put the class in groups of three. Each group has to write a research paper and make a presentation. Our group chooses to research Saudi Weddings.

Reflection

In this assignment, I took the role of the team leader by managing the roles of each member and how we work. I created a shared google document so we and work on the research remotely and each member wrote his part. Also, I assigned one of the members to do the presentation design layout and using Prezi. I learn how to set up an online environment so it became easier for the members to work from anywhere and whenever they can.
